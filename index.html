About Me

I’m 38 y.o. and I’m from Ukraine. I have been living in Denmark for half a year since the beginning of the war. In Ukraine, I worked for 14 years in a state institution in the field of land/cadastral resources. First as an HR but due to the circumstances in 2008, I was involved in the process of transferring information from physical sources to digital format (XML). It took over two years. I enjoyed this kind of work and I was offered the position of a cadastral registrar with the condition of studying at the university and obtaining a bachelor's degree in geodesy and land management. So, I worked and studied at the same time. During the period of COVID-19 lockdown and the availability of free time, I became interested in programming and web development. It fascinated me so much that I realized that I would like to do this all my life. I like the magic of creating something out of nothing....
The first learning platform was courses on Udemy, then a lot of video tutorials on YouTube. During my studying, I wanted to practice more, so my programmer friends gave me easy tasks to make websites, download pictures from the Figma and sort into folders, make small blocks layouts etc.

In autumn 2021 I entered The Rolling scope school from EPAM and successfully completed JS/FE PRE-SCHOOL 2022 (JAVASCRIPT). In Denmark, I applied and passed the selection to the coding school HackYourFuture and since October I have been studying there.

I live in Kolding and go to Sprogskole. I like Danish despite all its complexity of pronunciation and spelling but I have a good will to learn the language.

Navbar Effect | javaScript

              
                const fullImg = document.querySelector(".full-img");
                const smallImg = document.querySelectorAll(".gallery img");
                const modal = document.querySelector(".modal");
                
                // console.log(smallImg);
                smallImg.forEach(function (img) {
                  img.addEventListener("click", function () {
                    modal.classList.add("open");
                    fullImg.classList.add("open");
                
                    // Changin' the images dynamically
                    const originalQuality = img.getAttribute("alt");
                    fullImg.src = `img/full/${originalQuality}.jpg`;
                  });
                });
                
                modal.addEventListener("click", function (e) {
                  if (e.target.classList.contains("modal")) {
                    modal.classList.remove("open");
                    fullImg.classList.remove("open");
                  }
                });
